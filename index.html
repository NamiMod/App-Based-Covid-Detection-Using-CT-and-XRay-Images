Stop Corona Virus!

Unfortunately, the covid-19 pandemic has changed all aspects of our lives. since the first day of the epidemic, many people worldwide have tried to do something that can help others, like improving detection methods, making vaccines, and so on. These tries show that we can stop covid-19 or at least decrease the number of death.



How to be safe?!

There are some simple ways that you can protect yourself from covid-19.

  • Be sure to wash your hands when you touch things or use Disinfectants.
  • Please wear masks!
  • As far as possible, don't be in a crowded location.
  • Quarantine yourself when you found out that you got covid-19.


Vaccination

One of the important things that everyone should do is vaccination. with vaccines, not only you will be safe but also you can protect others from covid-19 and we can end the era of Covid-19.
Take it seriously!
